Reciepe for payasam(Kheer)

First Vermicelli should be fried using ghee,then we should pour some milk in a vessel and add 1 cup of the Vermicelli and we should add good amount sugar to it.
Then when it gets boiled we should pour rest of the milk in it and add some saffron and Cardamom.
Then In other vessel after frying dry grapes and cashews with Ghee and pour it to the payasam.
Then we can take a cup and drink it will be delicoous
